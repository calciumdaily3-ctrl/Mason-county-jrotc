About Our Program

The Mason County High School JROTC program is dedicated to developing citizenship, leadership, and personal responsibility in our cadets. Through military-based instruction and hands-on activities, we prepare students for success in college, careers, and life.

Established to serve the students of Mason County, Kentucky, our program emphasizes academic excellence, physical fitness, and community service while fostering a sense of pride and discipline.

🎯

Mission

To motivate young people to be better citizens through leadership education, character development, and community service.

👁️

Vision

Building the next generation of leaders through discipline, integrity, and excellence in all endeavors.

⭐

Values

Loyalty • Duty • Respect • Selfless Service • Honor • Integrity • Personal Courage

Cadet Activities

🏃

Physical Training

Regular PT sessions focusing on fitness, endurance, and team building activities that challenge cadets physically and mentally.

🎖️

Drill Team

Precision marching and rifle drill competitions at local, state, and national levels. Our drill team represents the best of military bearing.

🎯

Marksmanship

Air rifle training and competitions emphasizing safety, concentration, and precision shooting skills.

🤝

Community Service

Color guards for community events, veteran support activities, and local service projects that build civic responsibility.

🏕️

Leadership Camp

Summer leadership camps and field training exercises that develop outdoor skills and leadership under challenging conditions.

📚

Academic Teams

JROTC Academic Bowl and Leadership Bowl competitions testing knowledge of JROTC curriculum, current events, and leadership principles.
